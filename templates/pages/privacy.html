Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [15-01-2026]

At Small Online Tool ("we," "us," "our," or the "Website"), operating at smallonlinetool.com, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our free online tools.

1. Information We Collect

a) Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

Like most websites, Small Online Tool collects non-personally identifiable information automatically. This may include:

  • Your browser type and version.
  • Your device type and operating system.
  • The pages of our website that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and time spent on those pages.
  • Referring website addresses.
  • IP address (which may be considered personal data in some jurisdictions; we use it for analytics and security in a non-identifying way).

b) Data You Provide to Our Tools

The core function of Small Online Tool is to process user-inputted data (e.g., text, numbers, files) within your browser to provide instant tool results. We do not store, save, or permanently retain the data you input into the tools on smallonlinetool.com. Processing typically occurs client-side or is transient for the immediate operation. We do not mine or sell this data.

c) Personal Information (Voluntary)

We do not require accounts or registration to use the tools on Small Online Tool. We only collect personal information (like your email address) if you voluntarily provide it, for example, by contacting us through a form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the performance of Small Online Tool.
  • Analyze trends and user behavior to enhance your experience on smallonlinetool.com.
  • Ensure the security and stability of our website.
  • Fulfill our legitimate interests in website administration.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies. Data is aggregated and anonymous. You can opt-out via Google's tools.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and others. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • Amazon Associates: As noted in our Terms, Small Online Tool is an Amazon Associate. Amazon may use cookies to track referrals from our site for commission purposes. Please review Amazon's Privacy Notice.

We do not control the cookies of these third parties. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Small Online Tool uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information to improve functionality (e.g., user preferences) and for the advertising purposes described above. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures designed to protect the non-personal information we collect. However, no electronic transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: To request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification: To request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: To request deletion of your personal data.
  • Objection: To object to our processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us. We may need to verify your identity before responding. We do not process significant personal data, as use of our tools is anonymous.

7. Children's Privacy

Small Online Tool is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child provided data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy for Small Online Tool periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of smallonlinetool.com after changes constitutes acknowledgment and consent.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy for Small Online Tool, please contact us through the contact form available on smallonlinetool.com.
